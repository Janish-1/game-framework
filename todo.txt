User Logout:
router.post('/logout', logout);

User Deactivation:
router.delete('/users/:id', deactivateUser);

Search Users:
router.get('/users/search', searchUsers);

Notification Preferences:
router.put('/users/:id/notifications', updateNotificationPreferences);

User Activity Feed:
router.get('/users/:id/activity', getUserActivity);

Terms of Service and Privacy Policy:
router.get('/terms', getTermsOfService);
router.get('/privacy', getPrivacyPolicy);

Feedback or Contact Form:
router.post('/feedback', submitFeedback);

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
router.post('/users/:id/2fa/enable', enableTwoFactorAuth);
router.post('/users/:id/2fa/disable', disableTwoFactorAuth);
router.post('/users/:id/2fa/verify', verifyTwoFactorAuth);

Admin-only Endpoints:
router.get('/admin/users', getAllUsersForAdmin);
router.delete('/admin/users/:id', deleteUserByAdmin);
